Data Deletion

Delete your Remindly data

Last updated: June 12, 2026

Delete local app data

You can delete notes, tasks, and reminders inside the Remindly app. You can also delete local device data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage from Android system settings.

Request cloud data deletion

If you used cloud sync, sign-in, image upload, or premium account features, you can request deletion of your account-linked cloud data by emailing: deepaknehra416@gmail.com

Please include:

  • The email address or account identifier used in Remindly.
  • A clear request, such as "Please delete my Remindly account and cloud data."
  • Any extra detail needed to verify the account belongs to you.

What may be deleted

  • Account profile records associated with Remindly.
  • Cloud-synced notes, tasks, reminders, and related metadata.
  • Uploaded note images or assistant-related stored files, if any.

What may be retained

Some data may be retained for a limited time if required for security, fraud prevention, legal obligations, billing records, backup restoration windows, or app operations.

Processing time

We aim to process deletion requests within 30 days after verifying the request, unless a longer period is required or permitted by law.
