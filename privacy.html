Privacy Policy for Remindly

Last updated: June 12, 2026

Developer note: replace deepaknehra416@gmail.com with your real support email before publishing this page.

This Privacy Policy explains how Remindly collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the Remindly mobile app. Remindly helps users create and manage notes, tasks, reminders, alarms, assistant requests, and optional voice capture through Google Assistant or Gemini where available.

Information We Collect

Depending on which features you use, Remindly may collect or process:

  • Notes, tasks, reminders, reminder times, alarm settings, and completion history.
  • Voice command text received from Google Assistant or Gemini App Actions.
  • Text messages you send to the in-app AI assistant.
  • Photos or images you attach for note editing or assistant image reading.
  • Account information, such as email address or authentication identifiers, if cloud sync is enabled.
  • Purchase or subscription status if premium features are enabled.
  • Basic analytics events, crash logs, device information, and app diagnostics if those services are configured.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Create, save, edit, search, and sync your notes, tasks, reminders, and alarms.
  • Process assistant requests and convert natural language into app actions.
  • Handle Google Assistant or Gemini voice capture, such as adding a task or reminder by voice.
  • Upload and display note images when you choose to attach images.
  • Provide premium access, account sync, support, diagnostics, and app reliability improvements.

AI and Voice Features

If AI features are enabled, the text you enter, attached images, selected note context, or voice capture text may be sent to an AI processing service or to a developer-controlled AI proxy to generate a response or app action. Do not enter sensitive information into the AI assistant if you do not want it processed by those services.

Permissions

Remindly may request permissions only when needed for app features:

  • Notifications: to deliver reminders and alarms.
  • Microphone or voice-related access: to support voice capture or speech input where available.
  • Camera and photos: to attach images or let the assistant read an image you choose.
  • Wake lock or alarm-related Android access: to run reminder, alarm, or voice capture behavior reliably.

Storage and Third Parties

Remindly may store data locally on your device. If cloud sync or image upload is enabled, data may also be stored with backend providers such as Supabase. If configured, Remindly may use services such as OpenAI or an AI proxy for assistant features, PostHog for analytics, Sentry for crash reporting, RevenueCat for subscription status, and Google Play services for app distribution and billing-related platform features.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as needed to operate app features, comply with law, protect the app, or use service providers that process data on our behalf.

Data Retention

Local app data stays on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app. Cloud data, if enabled, is retained while your account is active or until you request deletion, subject to backup, security, legal, and operational requirements.

Your Choices

  • You can delete notes, tasks, and reminders inside the app.
  • You can disable permissions from Android system settings.
  • You can avoid AI features if you do not want assistant text or images processed externally.
  • You can request account or cloud data deletion using the Data Deletion page.

Children

Remindly is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and delete it where required.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
